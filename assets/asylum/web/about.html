Asylum is a podcast about, for and by the JBoss Community.

We try and cover all the news in the JBoss Community about new projects, releases, speaker events and if there are any general news that we find interesting to the community.

We also asks JBoss Community member to be on to the podcast to discuss his/her work and comment on the news.

If you like to be on the podcast and talk about your project then send a mail to asylum@jboss.org.
