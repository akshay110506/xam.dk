Welcome to the asylum
Welcome to the JBoss Community Asylum podcast.
This is a low-key experiment started by three JBoss employees: Max R Andersen (JBoss tooling), Emmanuel Bernard (Hibernate team) and Michael Neale (Drools and his head in the clouds).
The idea is to get a podcast focused on the JBoss Community and its projects: news, interviews etc.

We welcome feedback (cheer leaders accepted as well), leave a comment on this blog.

The first episode will be up soon, stay tuned.
